## ğŸ® How to Play

### Game Objective
One player receives a different word while all other players receive the same word. Players take turns describing their word without saying it explicitly. The goal is to:
- **For regular players**: Identify who has the different word (the spy)
- **For the spy**: Blend in and identify what the common word is

### Game Setup
1. **Select Number of Players**: Choose 3-8 players
2. **Choose a Category**: Select from various categories like Food, Animals, Technology, etc.
3. **Word Assignment**: Each player secretly views their assigned word
4. **Discussion**: Players take turns describing their word
5. **Voting**: Players vote on who they think is the spy
6. **Reveal**: The spy and their word are revealed

### Controls

#### Mobile/Touch Devices
- **Drag up** on the card to reveal your word
- **Tap** the card to toggle word visibility
- Use buttons to navigate between players

#### Desktop
- **Click** the card to toggle word visibility
- **Drag up** with mouse to reveal your word
- Use keyboard or mouse to navigate

## ğŸ­ Gameplay Tips

### For Regular Players
- Listen carefully to word descriptions
- Look for vague or unusual descriptions
- Note players who seem hesitant or confused
- Compare patterns in word associations

### For the Spy
- Listen to other players' descriptions carefully
- Try to guess the common word quickly
- Use general descriptions that could fit multiple words
- Don't be too specific or too vague

### General Strategy
- Keep descriptions concise (1-2 sentences)
- Avoid using the actual word or direct synonyms
- Pay attention to timing and confidence levels
- Use association and context clues
